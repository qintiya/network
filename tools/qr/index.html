Supported QR Code Data Formats

AWDev QR Code Generator supports five primary standard static QR formats optimized for mobile device scanning across Android and iOS platforms:

  • URL (Web Address): Instantly directs users to your website, landing page, social media profile, or online store.
  • VCard (Digital Business Card): Shares full personal or corporate contact details (Name, Mobile Number, Email, Company) that can be instantly saved into a phone's address book.
  • Text (Plain String): Encodes arbitrary text messages, Wi-Fi keys, coupon codes, or notes without requiring internet connectivity to read.
  • Email: Pre-fills a recipient email address, subject line, and body message directly in the user's default email client.
  • SMS: Pre-configures a target phone number and text payload to streamline mobile messaging campaigns.

Why Use High-Resolution Static QR Codes?

Static QR codes embed information directly into the matrix data pattern. Unlike dynamic links, static QR codes never expire, require no database tracking backend, and ensure maximum user privacy and lightning-fast scanning capabilities.
